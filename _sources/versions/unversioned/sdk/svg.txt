Svg
===


.. attribute:: Exponent.Components.Svg

   一些绘画原语, 比如: ``Circle``, ``Rect``, ``Path``,
   ``ClipPath``, 还有 ``Polygon``. 支持大多数 SVG 元素还有属性。
   实现是通过 `react-native-svg 4.1.3
   <https://github.com/magicismight/react-native-svg/tree/c9a64c44fcf48b57b2401925950befa3727deb24>`_,
   文档在: `<https://github.com/magicismight/react-native-svg/tree/c9a64c44fcf48b57b2401925950befa3727deb24>`_.
